The Founder's Story

At 16 years old youngest, Vedant Vhatkar has done what most people don't accomplish in a career — earning Hall of Fame recognition from 8 organisations, being acknowledged by the Government of India, competing in India's biggest live hacking event, and serving as a Brand Ambassador for HackWithIndia.

His philosophy: knowledge shared is never wasted. He's not just finding vulnerabilities — he's building toward a mission.
